श्री श्याम मंदिर, घिलोर कलां

Shri Shyam Mandir is a revered Hindu temple located in the village of Ghilor Kalan, Haryana. It is dedicated to Baba Khatushyam Ji, a manifestation of Barbarika from the Mahabharata, and serves as a major spiritual hub for devotees in the Rohtak district.

The temple is known for its welcoming atmosphere and is a focal point for local religious life, often drawing large crowds during special lunar occasions and festivals.

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance and dedicated parking lot
  • Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for prayers and darshan
  • A peaceful and divine place for meditation and seeking blessings
  • Located in Rohtak, part of a local pilgrimage circuit with nearby shrines
