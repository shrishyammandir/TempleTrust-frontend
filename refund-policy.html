Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy

All donations made to Shri Shyam Mandir are voluntary contributions and are generally non-refundable as they are utilized for religious and charitable activities.

Exceptions

Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • Duplicate payment made due to technical error
  • Incorrect amount charged due to system malfunction
  • Payment debited but donation not recorded

Refund Process

To request a refund, contact us within 7 days of the transaction at +91-9876543210 with:

  • Transaction ID
  • Receipt number (if available)
  • Reason for refund request
  • Bank statement showing the debit

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days. The amount will be credited to the original payment method.

Cancellation Policy

Once a donation is successfully processed, it cannot be cancelled. However, if you notice an error immediately, contact us at +91-9876543210.
